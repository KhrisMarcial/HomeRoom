About HomeRoom

Our mission is to make education accessible and empowering for students and teachers. We’ve made it our goal to provide an online classroom with a user friendly experience for students and teachers, enabling assignments to be distributed, managed and updated effectively and efficiently. The world is faced with a need for reliable and user-friendly educational software. Students aren’t able to sit face to face with teachers and teachers are forced implement lesson plans from afar.
